The Land of High Passes

My first trip to Ladakh was in 2008. Enchanted by this very fantastical trans-Himalayan region of India, by its untouched beauty and culture, its enormity of mountains and rivulets, my love for the place has only grown over the years.

Strange as it may seem I can't get out of this affair anymore.

I plan to visit again.

- Satie Sharma
